![](/img/sheila-mod-side-angle.jpg)

## My Approach

I am devoted to creating community through mind–body–soul connection.

Yoga, to me, is more than movement — it is a practice of remembering our wholeness. In my classes we explore strength, softness, curiosity, and play. I encourage students to listen deeply to their bodies while discovering new layers of resilience and creativity.

Whether you're brand new or a seasoned yogi, you are welcome exactly as you are.
