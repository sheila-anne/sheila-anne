# She Flows Camp

## Details

September 25th - 27th
Bethel Farm, Hillsborough NH

**Just imagine**

You turn onto a quiet road, trees stretching overhead, and your shoulders are already starting to drop. You arrive not knowing exactly what to expect but within minutes, you're laughing with women who feel familiar and gazing around at the grounds that embrace you warmly.

Soon enough, you’re jumping in the pond, warming up in the sauna, wandering to a waterfall, moving your body, telling stories by the fire with s’mores.

You’re resting when you need to, playing when you want to, and realizing, you don’t have to try so hard here. Somewhere between the movement, the stillness, and the in-between moments, you feel like yourself again. You belong to the earth, to this community, to yourself. And it’s everything.

Welcome to Camp She Flows. ✨

### What To Expect

She Flows Collective is leaving the Seacoast (don’t worry, it’s less than 90 minutes away!) for a weekend that will be like summer camp energy but make it nourishing, spacious, and deeply grounding.

Enjoy classes and workshops in Bethel Farm’s sun soaked studio that will invite you out of your busy mind and into your wise body. This includes vinyasa and restorative yoga, meditation and journaling, as well as group Reiki healing will untether you from stress and harmonize your energy.

Remember what it’s like to breathe deeply as you explore the grounds, walk barefoot to the pond, sweat it out in the woodfired sauna and wake up that alive feeling in your skin. There will be plenty of unstructured time to nap in the sun, wander, connect, or be silent. There is a short hike to a nearby waterfall and swimming hole too.

Find connection and feel belonging through play, evenings by the fire, sticky fingers from s’mores, stories spilling out under the stars.

We welcome all generations of women - you’re not too old to go to camp and create lifelong memories.

### What's Included

- Daily guided mindful movement (all levels, no experience needed)
- Farm-to-table meals
- Sauna + pond dips
- Campfire night with s’mores
- Group Reiki healing
- Daily journal prompts
- Unbothered time to rest, read, explore, and play
- Curated welcome bag featuring Seacoast goodies
- Pre-retreat + post-retreat community meetup
- Additional camp-style activities TBA

**You belong**

You don’t need to bring a group. You don’t need to know anyone. You don’t need to be at a certain place in your life. You’re not too old to come to camp!

This space is designed for real connection, the kind that happens when women gather with open hearts, zero expectations, and a backpack of responsibilities to untether from for the weekend.

You’ll be met exactly where you are.

### This is for you if…

You’ve been craving genuine connection

You want to get out of your routine and into nature

You miss play, laughter, and that carefree feeling

You’re ready to prioritize yourself (without guilt)

You want to feel part of something real

### Home for the weekend

Various options to suit any experience you desire! Bathrooms are shared and thoughtfully set up to support the rhythm of camp life. You’ll have access to indoor facilities, as well as outdoor showers, sinks, and composting toilets nestled in the woods.
