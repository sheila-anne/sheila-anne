### What To Expect

She Flows Collective is leaving the Seacoast (don’t worry, it’s less than 90 minutes away!) for a weekend that will be like summer camp energy but make it nourishing, spacious, and deeply grounding.

Enjoy classes and workshops in Bethel Farm’s sun soaked studio that will invite you out of your busy mind and into your wise body. This includes vinyasa and restorative yoga, meditation and journaling, as well as group Reiki healing will untether you from stress and harmonize your energy.

Remember what it’s like to breathe deeply as you explore the grounds, walk barefoot to the pond, sweat it out in the woodfired sauna and wake up that alive feeling in your skin. There will be plenty of unstructured time to nap in the sun, wander, connect, or be silent. There is a short hike to a nearby waterfall and swimming hole too.

Find connection and feel belonging through play, evenings by the fire, sticky fingers from s’mores, stories spilling out under the stars.

We welcome all generations of women - **you’re not too old to go to camp** - and create lifelong memories.

### Home for the weekend

![](/img/yoga-castle.jpg)

Bethel Farm 📍

Bethel Farm is a small yoga and living arts center tucked away in a beautiful, scenic wooded area. They focus on cultivating community and nourishing the whole human. You'll feel fully welcome and recharged as you freely explore the grounds, devour farm to table meals in the large dining hall, and mindfully move in the sun-soaked yoga castle.

![](/img/yoga-studio.jpg)

Various options to suit any experience you desire! Bathrooms are shared and thoughtfully set up to support the rhythm of camp life. You’ll have access to indoor facilities, as well as outdoor showers, sinks, and composting toilets nestled in the woods.

#### Sheila

Sheila is the founder of She Flows Collective as well as the owner of Sheila Anne Coaching & Wellness. When Sheila lived in Denver, Colorado, she struggled to create meaningful friendships until she discovered a women's wellness collective. It changed her life. When she moved home to New Hampshire, she knew she needed to create the same opportunities for women to gather, genuinely connect, and create lifelong memories. While, Sheila leads retreats nationally and internationally, she is buzzing with excitement to host this special She Flows retreat close to her hometown. In her classes, Sheila invites individuals to step onto their mats to explore not only poses, but also their innate strength, creativity, and beautiful humanness. She is also a professional coach for ambitious women, Reiki Level 2 practitioner, and Human Design reader.

![](/img/camp-she-flows-erin.jpg)

#### Erin

Erin is the Event Coordinator for the She Flows Wellness Collective as well as Studio Manager and Yoga Teacher at 3 Bridges Yoga.
