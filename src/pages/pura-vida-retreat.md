![](/img/sheila-krysta-meet-hosts.jpg "Meet your hosts")

**Sheila Anne** of Sheila Anne Coaching + Wellness

Sheila visited Costa Rica for the first time in 2014 (where she also connected with long lost family!) and each time she returns feels like a special homecoming. Her favorite part of the annual retreat is getting to know individuals on a deeper level and experiencing transformational practices together like those from guest yoga teachers and time spent out in nature! Sheila received her 200 hour yoga teacher certification with Krysta-Lyn at South Boston Yoga. She actually missed the first certification weekend for her mother’s funeral service, so the training came at a potent time of healing and self evolution. She and Krysta-Lyn were meant to connect and collaborate due to their shared pasts and future goals! Sheila became an ICF Professional Certified Coach and works with individuals, groups, and businesses. In the last few years, she has added a divine play practice she calls “Lila” to her repertoire , as well as Reiki energy healing. She also founded a local women’s wellness collective (She Flows Collective) in Seacoast NH. She believes that when we come home to ourselves we transform our lives

**Krysta-Lyn** of [Radiate Wellness](https://www.radiatewellness.co/)

Krysta-Lyn found refuge in the combination of yoga, counseling and community after the loss of her mother in 2013. Overcome by the immense sense of peace yoga could provide, she immersed herself in her practice learning that life truly exists between the inhale and exhale. Krysta-Lyn sought to share her passion and received her 200 hour yoga teacher certification from South Boston Yoga in 2018 (where she met Sheila). In 2019 she left the world of finance to seek a healthy, balanced career and life of healing. She earned a Reiki Masters Certification and a Masters of Science in Mental Health Counseling in May 2022. Her dearest friends call her their sunshine and she hopes to help others find what makes them radiate! Costa Rica is Krysta-Lyn’s home away from home. A place where you immediately feel at peace which provides a conducive environment for healing, connection and fun!

## Our Home

Our retreat will take place at a hotel that is 100% Costa Rican managed and owned. Created by three generations of strong, hardworking Tico women—Grandma, Mom, and daughter—you will feel their care and dedication throughout the property.

The hotel sits on a peaceful 9-acre property surrounded by tropical dry forest, where charming bungalows are nestled within nature. The land is home to an abundance of wildlife, and it’s common to see monkeys, iguanas, colorful tropical birds, squirrels, raccoons, and many other native species right on the grounds.

Located in the stunning Guanacaste province of Costa Rica, the retreat center is ideally positioned for exploration. The property is surrounded by several of the region’s most beautiful beaches, all just a short drive away, allowing guests to experience a variety of coastal landscapes rather than staying in just one beach town.

During the retreat, you’ll have the opportunity to immerse yourself in nature, relax to the sound of birdsong, explore nearby beaches, and experience the incredible natural beauty that makes this region of Costa Rica so special.
